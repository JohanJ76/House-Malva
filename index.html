Welcome to House Malva!

A Holiday home in Dana Bay - Western Cape, South Africa - offering 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, living room, dining room and kitchen. Guests also have access to a private BBQ area and secure off-street parking.

Safe secure neighbourhood. Also part of a conservancy with free roaming wild buck and lots of birding. Close to beaches in Dana Bay and walking distance to shopping centre.

You have the place to yourself!
The owner/manager is in an adjacent structure.
